About me

Enthusiast of education industry with a demonstrated trajectory in management consulting and EdTech, I am skilled in strategy consulting, customer development, and financial analysis. Currently, I am building my technical knowledge in web development to have a deeper understanding of how technology helps students, educators, and other stakeholders in the education sector.

I earned my MBA with Distinction from the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University in 2020, where I majored in marketing, finance, and management science. While at Kellogg, I co-founded an education startup exploring innovative ways of delivering STEM project-based curricula. In addition, I was awarded the McGowan Fellowship sponsored by the William G. McGowan Charitable Fund as the only recipient in my MBA class.

Prior to Kellogg, I was a professional specialized in transaction advisory at KPMG. I served public multinational companies spanning across tech, auto, and manufacturing sectors on their M&A, IPO, and accounting change projects. I began my career at Deloitte in the Audit practice.

In addition to my MBA from Kellogg School of Management, I hold a Master of Accounting degree from Ross School of Business at University of Michigan, and Bachelor of Economics and Bachelor of Science in Psychology degrees from Peking University. Originally from Beijing, China, I am fluent in Mandarin. In my spare time, I like to read, travel, sing, and play with my cat Boba.
